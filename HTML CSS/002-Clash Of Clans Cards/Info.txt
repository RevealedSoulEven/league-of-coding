Barbarian Detail
The Barbarian is a single target melee troop. He is the first troop unlocked in the Barracks. He appears as a male kilt-clad warrior with an angry, battle-ready expression, hungry for destruction. He has close-cropped blonde hair and a long, yellow horseshoe mustache.
